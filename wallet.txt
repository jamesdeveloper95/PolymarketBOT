# Wallet setup — what to put here so the bot can connect and trade

1. Add your Polymarket wallet private key or mnemonic phrase below.
   - Private key: one line starting with 0x (from MetaMask: Account details → Export Private Key).
   - Mnemonic: 12 or 24 words from your wallet backup.

2. Save this file. Do not share it or commit it to a public repo.

3. Run the bot; it will read this file and connect to Polymarket to place trades.
